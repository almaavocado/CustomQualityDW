About

Who we are.

Custom Quality Drywall Inc. is based in Los Angeles with over 14 years of experience; carefully listening and getting to know our clients helps us to provide quality drywall and stud framing services for their unique residential and commercial projects. We are committed to ensure quality performance with sustained hard work, relentless attention to details and using the best material to build a strong and safe future in the Los Angeles County, Orange County, Ventura County and through out Southern California.

Founder

Meet the Custom Quality Drywall Inc. Owner.

Edgar Encarnacion

Edgar Encarnacion has over 14 years of experience with drywall services. His expertise started at the age of 16; during his High School years he worked part-time at his dad’s drywall business, engaging in projects where he closely developed quality hands on experience. This led him to pursue a career in drywall with a strong belief of quality over quantity. His passion for personal-growth and construction helped him acquire his C9 Drywall Contractors License in 2016. Over the years, he has established himself as a hard working, attentive, detail-oriented professional who has become an asset to many companies in the Southern California area. Whether he is needed for short-term or long-term projects, he has structured his company in such a way that each client feels as they are his only client. His dedication has led him to built a trust worthy company where he mentors 12 employees to provide the best service to the clients.

Taping

Drywall seams need to have taped embedded in joint compound. CQ Drywall Inc. provides with two unique finishes; smooth finish level 4 and skim coat level 5. This ensures a beautiful smooth wall.

Drywall

Construction material used to create walls and ceilings. CQ Drywall Inc. creates many unique design features, including arches and other architectural specialties.

Stud Framing

Building a strong foundation starts with the most common component of a wall panel. Studs structure and offer much of the support for a wall panel. CQ Drywall Inc. ensures to provide the best material for a strong support.

Shadow Mold Trim

These wall moldings are an important detail in the design and installation. CQ Drywall Inc. creates an aesthetic reveal between drywall and dissimilar material including door and window frames.

Fry Reglet Trim

CQ Drywall Inc. uses quality technique to shape and sculpt interior drywall partitions, make seamless transitions, and connect dissimilar wall and ceiling finishes and base boards.
